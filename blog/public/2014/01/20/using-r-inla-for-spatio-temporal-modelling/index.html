Using R-INLA for spatio-temporal modelling

January 20 2014
bayesian statistics blog Blog science spatial statistics statistics

Every once in a while I have to figure out how to do something in R-INLA. The easiest thing to do, obviously, is to see if anyone else has done the same thing before. Often they have and I am continually impressed by what INLA is capable of doing. I’m currently revising my paper on spatio-temporal modelling of outdoor PNC in the UPTECH project and the following papers have been incredibly helpful:

  • Krainski and Lindgren (2013) - The R-INLA tutorial: SPDE models
  • Martins, Simpson, Lindgren and Rue (2013) - Bayesian computing with INLA: new features (or there’s the arXived version)
  • Blangiardo, Cameletti, Baio and Rue (2013) - Spatial and spatio-temporal models with R-INLA (tech report version)
  • Cameletti, Lindgren, Simpson and Rue (2013) - Spatio-temporal modeling of particulate matter concentration through the SPDE approach (tech report version)
