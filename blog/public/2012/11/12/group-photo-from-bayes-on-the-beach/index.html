November 12 2012

Attendees of the 2012 Bayes on the Beach meeting. Photo credit: Luisa Hall (photographer, pictured far left) and Sam Clifford (photo editor, third column of people, second from back). This photo is a composite of a photo taken by Luisa (without her in it) and a photo taken by the venue staff (with Luisa in it). The keynote speakers were Matt Wand (front row, yellow shirt), Kim-Anh Do (second row, second from right) and Dave Woods (not picutred). Murray Aitkin is standing up the back. The conference organisers were Professor Kerrie Mengersen (third from the front on the far right), Nicole White (second from left in the floral dress), Jannah Baker (second row, fifth from left), Matt Moores (far right up the back) and Dow Jaemjamrat (not pictured).
