Every once in a while a post will pop up in my RSS reader that makes me appreciate science as the creative domain of nerds. This time around, it’s a bunch of researchers hooking an iPod up to a squid in order to examine how its chromatophores (colour change bits) react to external electrical stimulus (in this case, Cypress Hill’s hit “Insane in the Membrane”).
